biography

Teat began his football career with youth teams in Belgium, including KAS Eupen, KRC Genk and Standard Liège. In 2020, he signed his first professional contract with KV Oostende, where he quickly became a key player, making 35 appearances and scoring 5 goals in his debut season.

In August 2021, he joined Italian club Bologna FC on loan, which was later bought. However, just four weeks later, he was sold to French club Stade Rennais for €19 million. Wikipedia – Die freie Enzyklopädie In August 2024, Theat joined Eintracht Frankfurt on loan, with a buyout option that was activated in February 2025.

Teat represented Belgium at various youth levels from 2015 to 2021, making 21 appearances. He made his senior debut on 16 November 2021 in a World Cup qualifier against Wales, playing 85 minutes. He played in all eight of Belgiums 2024 UEFA European Championship qualifiers, helping them qualify for the tournament unbeaten. At the European Championship itself, he played in three of his team's four matches.
